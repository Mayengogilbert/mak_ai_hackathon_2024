Makerere Lab AI Hackathon 2024
Makerere University
22 - 26 April 2024
Code. Create. Innovate.
The hackathon will focus on building machine learning solutions based on datasets Crop Image and spectral and Coffee and Cashew nut that were collected by the Makerere AI Lab as part of the Lacuna Project.

The Hackathon will target university students (undergraduate and graduate) who will be mentored and compete in teams to build machine learning solutions around some of the selected datasets
Meet the Organisers

Makerere AI Lab
The Artificial Intelligence and Data Science lab specialises in the application of artificial intelligence and data science -including, for example, methods from computer vision, natural language processing and predictive analytics-to problems in the developing world.
